Our Story

Kapehan ni Jai was founded by a friends whose shared love for coffee turned into a passion project. The name “Jai” is a combination of the founders’ names Jemuel, Anthea, and Isabel that symbolized their teamwork and friendship. What started as casual after-class hangouts over cups of homemade brew soon grew into an idea to create a cozy space where people could connect, relax, and enjoy quality coffee. Combining their different skills from crafting the perfect roast to designing a welcoming ambiance, they built Kapehan ni Jai as more than just a café, but a place where friendships are strengthened and new stories are brewed every day.
